# Sales Data Analysis Report

## Objective

Analyze sales data using Python and Pandas to discover useful business insights.

---

## Dataset

Columns:

- Date
- Product
- Quantity
- Price
- Total_Sales

---

## Data Cleaning

- Loaded CSV using pandas
- Filled missing Quantity values using median
- Calculated missing Total Sales
- Removed duplicate records

---

## Metrics Calculated

### 1. Total Revenue

Total revenue generated from all sales.

### 2. Total Quantity Sold

Total number of products sold.

### 3. Average Sale

Average revenue per transaction.

### 4. Best Selling Product

Product with highest total quantity sold.

### 5. Revenue by Product

Revenue generated by each product.

---

## Key Insights

- Laptop generated the highest revenue.
- Mouse sold the highest number of units.
- Missing values were successfully handled.
- Dataset was cleaned before analysis.
